Mac and Cheese

Description

Feel like a kid again with this simple, but tasty dish.

Ingredients

  • Macaroni
  • Cheese
  • Water

Steps

  1. Place water in a pot and bring to a boil.
  2. Add macaroni to boiling water and cook for 8 minutes.
  3. Drain water.
  4. Add cheese, making sure that it melts thoroughly.
